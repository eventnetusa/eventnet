VirtualSalesRooms is an original online platform designed to connect pre-qualified customers in video meetings with client’s sales representatives.

The bar has been raised in lead generation as consumers seeking your product initiate their meetings!

Unlimited scalability to enroll 10 to 10,000+ on-call representatives nationwide working from home or anywhere on mobile devices.

VirtualSalesRooms is a powerful asset for broker and agency recruitment.

Customer Requests Meeting

Customer initiates “Meet-Now” request from Google Ad, client website. social media or other advertising locations

Requests are Routed to Advisors

Customer enters home Zip Code and Preferred Language. Platform will search, identify and alert sales representatives, who meet requested location and language

Customer & Advisor Instantly Video Connect

1st Advisor to accept meeting is video connected to customer within seconds.
